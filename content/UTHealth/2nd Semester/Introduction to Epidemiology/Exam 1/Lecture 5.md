# Study Designs/Randomized Controlled Trials
- **Descriptive**: Doesnt start from hypothesis
- **Analytic**: Starts from Hypothesis

## Randomized Controlled Trials
 - Randomly assigns subject 
	 - new therapy v old therapy

Observational Study 
- Has choice
- Measures incidence
Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT)
- has no choice 
- measures incidence

**Blinding**
- Only subjects blind (Single)
- Both subject and researcher (Double)
- Subjects, researchers, and data analysts (Triple)

Simple RCT
- Just split into treatment and placebo

Factorial RCT
- One split then a second
- Test combo of drugs

Cross-over 
- Treatment -> wait -> nothing (same person)
- Nothing -> wait -> treatment (same person)

**Analysis strategy** 
- Effectiveness
	- How they were randomized
- Efficacy
	- What treatment they actually took
