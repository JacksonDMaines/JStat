# Validity and Reliability of Diagnostic Test

**Direct vs Indirect Standardization**
- Direct from standard population
- Indirect from subpopulation

**Validity:**
- Sens: $T^+|D^+$
- Spec: $T^-|D^-$
- NPV: $D^-|T^-$
- PPV: $D^+|T^+$

**Reliability:**
- Percent agreement: $\frac{\text{Correct}}{\text{Total}}$
- Kappa Statistics: Agreement among different tests
$$
\frac{\text{\% agreed} - \text{\% by chance}}{\text{100\%} -\text{\% by chance} }
$$
