# Normality of Data
## Graphical
	- Histogram
	- QQ-Plot
		- Quantile-Quantile
	- PP-Plot
		- Probability-Probability

For QQ-Plots and PP-Plots data needs to follow 45 degree angle

## Formal Tests
**Shapiro-Wilks test**
- $H_{0}$: Sample comes from normal distribution
- $H_{1}$: Sample did not come from normal distribution. 

Better when $n$ is small


## Transformations
 - Log, Sqrt, Square, Inverse, ..... 
 - This becomes difficult to interpret, but can help data be more normal like.
