# Paired Numerical Data
- **Parameter of Interest:** True population mean of differences $\mu_{d}/\delta$ 
- **Point Estimate:** Sample Mean of differences ($\bar{x}_{d}$)
- **Standard Error:** $s_{d}/\sqrt{ n }$ , (Note: this is SD of the differences)
- **Test Statistic:**
$$
t = \frac{\bar{x}_{d}-\delta_{0}}{s_{d}/\sqrt{ n }}
$$

- **Confidence Interval:**
$$

\bar{x}_{d}\pm (critial\space value)\cdot \frac{s_{d}}{\sqrt{ n }}
$$

**Note:** Basically this is the same as a one-sample test, but you use the differences between 2 groups as the data.
